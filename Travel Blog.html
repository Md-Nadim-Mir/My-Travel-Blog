About Saintmartin

Saint Martin, also known as Sint Maarten, is an island located in the northeast Caribbean Sea. It is one of the smallest inhabited islands divided between two nations: the French overseas collectivity of Saint Martin in the north, and the Kingdom of the Netherlands' constituent country of Sint Maarten in the south. The island covers an area of approximately 87 square kilometers (34 square miles). 


### Geography:
- **Location**: Saint Martin/Sint Maarten is situated approximately 240 kilometers (150 miles) east of Puerto Rico.
- **Terrain**: The island features hilly terrain with rugged coastline and stunning beaches.
- **Climate**: It has a tropical climate with relatively consistent temperatures year-round, moderated by trade winds.


### Economy:
- **Tourism**: Saint Martin/Sint Maarten is a popular tourist destination known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and duty-free shopping.
- **Trade**: The island's strategic location has historically made it a hub for trade in the Caribbean.
- **Services Sector**: Tourism, hospitality, and related services form a significant part of the economy.
